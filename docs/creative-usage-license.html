(Last Updated: 21 January 2022)

WHERE AS, the "PET" is the digital asset associated with your token. NOW THEREFORE, You have broad rights to use the PET. You can do any of the following:
Use the PET for your own personal use; Use the PET when you are on a marketplace that allows the purchase and sale of your PET, so long as the marketplace cryptographically verifies that you are the owner; Use the PET when you’re on a third party website or app that allows the inclusion, involvement, or participation of your PET, so long as the website/app cryptographically verifies that you are the owner, and the PET doesn’t stay on the website/app if you close your account; and Use the PET to commercialize your own merchandise, provided that you aren’t earning more than $100,000 in revenue each year from doing so.
There are a few things that aren’t appropriate uses for the PET. They include:
Modifying the PET; Using the PET to market or sell third-party products; Using the PET in connection with images of hatred, violence, or other inappropriate behavior; or Trying to trademark the PET, or otherwise acquire intellectual property rights in it.
